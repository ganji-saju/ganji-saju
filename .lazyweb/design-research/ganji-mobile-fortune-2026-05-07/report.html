Lazyweb Design Research: Ganji Subpages

Scope is design-only: no home changes, no color-system changes, and no saju RAG/content changes.

Reference Directions

Daily insight apps

Calm, Headspace, Breeze: short daily cards and clear bottom navigation.

Score/result apps

Gentler, Oura, Apple Fitness: one primary result followed by compact metric cards.

Checkout apps

Adidas, Walmart, Best Buy: product summary, method rows, and one strong CTA.

Saved/list apps

AllTrails, Artsy, Nike: roomy tappable rows with simple filters.

Applied Guardrails

  • Keep the existing white, black, and pink palette.
  • Keep the current home screen untouched.
  • Keep all saju interpretation/RAG/data logic untouched.
  • Polish only subpage surfaces, spacing, tabs, rows, and payment/result hierarchy.
